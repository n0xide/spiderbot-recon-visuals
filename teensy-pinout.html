free pins

Headroom for expansion

Digital GPIO free: 12, 26, 27, 31, 32, 38–41 (8 pins · ADC capable)

Analog inputs available: A14–A17 (pins 38–41) for additional sensors

UARTs available: Serial3 (pins 14/15 conflict w/ FSR), Serial4 (16/17 conflict), Serial5 (20/21 conflict), Serial6 (24/25 conflict), Serial7 (28/29 conflict), Serial8 (34/35 conflict). All higher UARTs collide with current assignments — plan early if more serial needed.

CAN: pin 22/23 (FlexCAN1) — currently FSR. CAN2 on pin 0/1 — currently bus. CAN3 on pin 30/31 — pin 30 INA226 ALERT. CAN requires reassignment.

Signal integrity rules of thumb (schematic.md §7)

Servo bus

DATA line ≤80 cm total. Twisted-pair with GND if longer. 120 Ω termination at far end of each sub-bus if scope shows ringing.

I²C

4.7 kΩ pull-ups, ≤30 cm total run, 100 kHz initially, bump to 400 kHz only after bench validation.

CSI camera ribbon

Route flat, away from servo motors and 12 V rail. Magnetic interference from servo coils corrupts the CSI clock under a servo.

LoRa antenna

SMA pigtail ≤10 cm. RYLR998's quarter-wave omni doesn't need an explicit ground plane.
