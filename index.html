What you're building

An 8-legged, carbon-fiber-nylon, sensor-laden walking robot piloted from a homebuilt cyberdeck. Industrial-prototype-grade: hot-swap legs, NATO accessory rail, smart power sequencing, conformal coating, IP54-target sealing. ROS 2 Humble on Jetson Orin Nano, 1 kHz IK on Teensy 4.1, LoRa C2 over Pico 2 W. Walks, sees, maps, hears, and talks back.

Default octopod (8 legs). Override to hexapod saves ~$300 and 25% software work.
